About Me

Age: 28 - Pronouns: She/Her
Originally from North Sydney, Cape Breton.
Moved to Halifax in April 2019 to live with my best friend of a decade and partner of 5 years, Patrick.

In my free time I enjoy embroidery, researching geological data, spending time with my significant other, and hanging out with our asthmatic orange cat, Louie.

Other than taking advantage of relaxing at home when I can, I enjoy walking along the Bedford waterfront to take in the fresh air and salt water. Two of my favourite bands are Mumford & Sons and Peach Pit, but I'll listen to just about anything. Newfie music will always have a special place in my heart. We traveled to PEI this July to see Mumford & Sons perform live, and I'd do it all over again in a heartbeat. I don't travel much, and have never left the country, but hopefully a change of career and an improved work-life balance will allow us to continue experiencing more of the world. A dream of mine would be to travel to Hawaii to visit Mount Kilauea, and Iceland.

After a lot of contemplation over the past couple of years, I decided to return to school for a change of career. With the support of many great people in my life, I learned the importance of prioritizing my physical and mental health, and have become an advocate for self-care and safety. It's been bittersweet leaving my profession, but I'm motivated and excited to learn new skills and apply them in my future career.

If you can't get a hold of me, I'm probably on FaceTime with my mom.
