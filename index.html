Marine Protection and Planning

Ecology Action Centre

Protecting marine life in Nova Scotia is a cause that matters for all of us.
Nova Scotia is my home and always will be, and that's why I care about sustaining our ocean environment. We're surrounded by it!
The coastal waters here are home to a diverse range of marine species, and keeping them safe ensures a thriving environment for everyone. It's not just about the species, this supports our communities that rely on healthy waters for our economic development. Healthy oceans help fight climate change by absorbing carbon and regulating the climate. Supporting marine life protection in Nova Scotia isn't just a local interest; it's a global responsibility.
It's about preserving our oceans, our communities, and a natural legacy for future generations to thrive.
Research and regulations are imperative to improving and sustaining marine livelihood.
